Danny Gelman - My Story

My Education Background

In 10th grade, I enrolled at ORT Telnof Airforce school with a passion for technology and a keen interest
in electricity. There, I chose to specialize in industrial electricity and aircraft electricity, eager to gain
practical knowledge and hands-on experience in these fields. Throughout my time at ORT Telnof, I immersed myself
in the study of electrical systems, learning about their components, functions, and maintenance.

I delved into topics such as circuitry, power distribution, and electrical safety protocols. The school provided an environment
that fostered both theoretical learning and practical application, ensuring a comprehensive understanding of the
subject matter. To further enhance my knowledge and gain real-world exposure, I sought opportunities beyond the
classroom. I decided to work as an IDF Civilian Worker, seizing the chance to apply my skills in a professional
setting. This experience allowed me to collaborate with skilled technicians, engineers, and military personnel,
contributing to various projects and gaining valuable insights into the intricacies of electrical systems within
the defense industry.

During my time as an IDF Civilian Worker, I had the opportunity to work on a range of assignments, from
troubleshooting electrical issues in aircraft to performing maintenance on critical infrastructure.
I honed my problem-solving abilities, adapting to new challenges and finding innovative solutions. The fast-paced and
demanding nature of the work instilled in me a strong sense of responsibility and discipline.
Additionally, I participated in specialized training programs that further expanded my expertise. I received comprehensive
instruction in advanced electrical systems specific to aircraft, acquiring a deep understanding of avionics, navigation systems,
and communication equipment.

This training exposed me to cutting-edge technologies and instilled in me the importance of precision and
attention to detail when working in high-stakes environments. Through my journey at ORT Telnof Airforce school and
my role as an IDF Civilian Worker, I not only developed a strong foundation in industrial and aircraft electricity
but also fostered essential skills such as teamwork, adaptability, and effective communication. These experiences
solidified my passion for technology and ignited a drive to pursue a career that combines my technical expertise with
my desire to make a positive impact. Now, armed with a wealth of knowledge and practical experience, I am ready to
embark on new challenges, leveraging my skills as I continue to grow professionally in the field of Technical Writing,
and obtaining new knowledge from courses I have completed such as the Software Manual Testing Course and the Fullstack
Web Development Bootcamp Course by Angela Yu.
